Privacy Policy - TikTok Viral Clothing Bot

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TikTok Viral Clothing Bot ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects data when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only publicly available data from TikTok:

  • Video Metadata: Views, likes, shares, comment counts
  • Public Comments: Publicly posted comments on videos
  • Video Information: Video links, thumbnails, descriptions
  • Hashtag Data: Public hashtags used in videos
  • Timestamps: When videos were posted

3. Information We DO NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
  • User email addresses or phone numbers
  • Private messages or direct messages
  • User passwords or credentials
  • Private or restricted content
  • Location data
  • Device information

4. How We Use Data

Collected data is used for:

  • Analyzing fashion and clothing trends
  • Identifying popular products and styles
  • Detecting product inquiry patterns
  • Business intelligence for e-commerce
  • Generating analytics reports

5. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally in SQLite database
  • No cloud storage of collected data
  • No external database services used
  • Data is encrypted at rest

6. Data Retention

  • Video data is automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • No long-term storage of TikTok content
  • Regular automated cleanup processes

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Share data with third parties
  • Sell or monetize collected data
  • Transfer data to external services
  • Use data for advertising purposes

8. TikTok API Compliance

  • We use TikTok's official API when available
  • We comply with TikTok's rate limits
  • We respect TikTok's Terms of Service
  • We only access publicly available content

9. User Rights

  • Contact us with privacy concerns

10. GDPR Compliance
